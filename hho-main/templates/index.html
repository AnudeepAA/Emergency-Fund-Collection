Welcome to Helping Hand

Your safety net in times of medical emergency

About Helping Hand

Helping Hand is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting college students and parents during medical emergencies. We believe that no one should have to choose between their education and their health. Our mission is to provide financial assistance and resources to those in need, ensuring that unexpected medical expenses don’t derail the future of our beneficiaries.

Our Services

At Helping Hand, we offer various forms of support to ease the burden of medical expenses:

  • Financial Assistance: We provide direct financial aid to cover medical bills, prescriptions, and other related expenses.
  • Resource Guidance: We connect students and parents with additional resources and services that can help them manage their health and finances.
  • Emergency Fundraising: We assist in setting up crowdfunding campaigns to gather additional support from communities and networks.
  • Advocacy: We advocate for policies and practices that reduce the financial strain of medical emergencies on students and families.
